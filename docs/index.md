# Kanji Builder

A kanji puzzle app for foreign Japanese learners. Build kanji from radicals, then words from kanji.

## About

Kanji Builder is an independent project built on top of openly licensed Japanese language data: KANJIDIC2 (CC BY-SA 4.0), KRADFILE, [davidluzgouveia/kanji-data](https://github.com/davidluzgouveia/kanji-data) (MIT), and [jamsinclair/open-anki-jlpt-decks](https://github.com/jamsinclair/open-anki-jlpt-decks) (MIT). See the in-app Acknowledgements screen for the full credit list.

This site exists primarily to host the legal pages above as stable URLs that the App Store and Google Play submission forms can point to.
